Our Products

We manufacture a wide variety of corrugated boxes to suit any application, from lightweight shipping to heavy-duty industrial use.

Regular Slotted Cartons (RSC)

The most common and versatile box style. Ideal for shipping, moving, and storage. All flaps are the same length, and the two outer flaps meet at the center.

Die-Cut Boxes

Custom-designed for specific products. Perfect for mailers, retail displays, or packaging with unique shapes and locking features. Highly precise and professional.

Heavy-Duty & Double Wall

Engineered for maximum protection. Made with double or triple-wall corrugated board, these are ideal for heavy items, fragile goods, and export shipping.

Full Overlap Cartons (FOL)

The outer flaps fully overlap, providing extra thickness and support on the top and bottom. Excellent for heavy items that need a stable, rigid base.

Custom Printed Boxes

Turn your shipping box into a marketing tool. We offer high-quality flexographic printing to add your logo, branding, and handling instructions.

Pads, Partitions & Sheets

Protect and organize products within your boxes. We create custom inserts, dividers, and pads to cushion and separate your items during transit.
